How to delete your Wavy account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. Here's what's removed and how to do it.

What gets deleted

  • Entire profile information (name, photos, bio)
  • Chat messages and conversation history
  • Matches, waves, and connections
  • Location data
  • Subscription information
  • Push notification tokens

Delete from the app

  1. Open the Wavy app
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Type DELETE to confirm
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted

Prefer we do it?

Email us at support@wavydating.com from (or mentioning) the phone number associated with your account, and we'll process your deletion request within 30 days.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your profile, matches, and messages cannot be recovered.
