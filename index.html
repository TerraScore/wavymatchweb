Feature 02 — Spots

Drop a Spot.
Gather your people.

Create a location-based Spot and give it a name — say, Thirdwave. Everyone who joins can see each other and connect. Make it public so anyone nearby can hop in, or private and approve each request yourself.

  • Always on. No countdown, no expiry — your Spot stays live for as long as you want it.
  • Public or private. Open the door to everyone nearby, or hand-pick who gets in.
  • Everyone connects. Anyone in the Spot can see who else is there on the map and start a chat.
