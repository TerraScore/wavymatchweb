How should the people in the café be represented?

This is the biggest visual decision — it sets the tone for every scene.

ABSTRACT · ORBS · MINIMAL

Glowing Presence Orbs

People = glowing circles. Isolated = dim/dark. Connected = lit up in rose. Elegant, modern, matches Wavy's digital-first vibe. Easiest to build in code.

SILHOUETTE · HUMAN · CINEMATIC

Silhouette Figures

Dark human shapes — recognisable as people but not detailed. The protagonist glows rose when they create the Spot. Others light up as they join. Emotionally resonant.

JUST JOINED
ILLUSTRATED · EXPRESSIVE · WARM

Illustrated Characters

Stylised faces with expressions — initially blank/bored, then smiling as connection happens. More work to animate but delivers the most emotional payoff.
