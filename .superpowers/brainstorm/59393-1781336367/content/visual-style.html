What visual style should the video use?

Each direction has a very different feel — pick the one that fits Wavy's brand best.

BOLD · FLAT · 2D

Flat Motion Graphics

Stylized 2D characters, Wavy's rose + blue palette, bold shapes. Think Headspace or Notion explainer. Buildable in Remotion with CSS.

CINEMATIC · DARK · GLOWING

Cinematic Dark

Dark backgrounds with glowing rose halos, depth-of-field blur on background characters. Matches Wavy's #0B0712 dark brand. Premium Spotify/Apple feel.

APP-FIRST · UI DRIVEN · CLEAN

App-Centric UI

The Wavy app is the hero — animated phone mockups show the Spots flow. Context (café scene) lives around it. Think Apple product video meets explainer.

You can also mix styles — e.g. Cinematic Dark for the café scene + App-Centric for the product moment.
